Registration for the 2021 season is currently open ONLY FOR RETURNING MEMBERS until Nov 30th. The club is limiting classes to 10 members per instructor in line with Cross Country Ontario guidelines. If there are available spaces after Nov 30th, these will be open to new registrants on a first come, first served basis. See the Registration page for details.
The club expects very strong interest this year, and will not be able to accommodate all registration requests.

The ONSC program normally runs from January to March (final date is weather/snow condition dependent) Saturdays at 10:00 am at the Hornet’s Nest (click here for a map).
