2025 season

Registration for the 2025 season is open, access it here.

2025 fees:

  • Individual $110.00
  • Family $250.00 (Family includes 2 adults and 4 children under the age of 18)

Please note that we only accept online registration.

The season is weather dependent but is usually 11 weeks (there may be an outing to the Gatineau Park one week) . 

Why not join us and see how fun winter can be?

Becoming a member too much of a commitment? Why not contribute by becoming a Sustaining Member (the amount is up to you).  The fee (payable by email transfer to This email address is being protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it.) gives you a membership card valid for ski shop discounts and it contributes to the survival of the club and the track setting at the Hornets Nest, Pine Grove Forest and Mer Bleue that many of us enjoy. It is very expensive to run the skidoo and other equipment – it costs approximately $50 in gas and oil for each outing.
